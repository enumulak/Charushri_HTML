About Me

Being a strong believer of Humanistic & Existential schools of Psychology, I have always worked in a client-centered approach and maintained that all individuals are capable of coping with their own life problems and achieve their 'Ideal-Self' by following their inner guidance. In this journey of self-actualization, we sometimes need a good listener and a facilitator to help that inner voice reach us.

Whatever I have learnt and known about becoming that facilitator, is being an unconditional support and a bridge connecting the inner (subconscious) and this outer (conscious) mind. With these two mental worlds coming together and working as one, a person can enjoy a fully functioning, peaceful living and conquer all walks of life more effectively and efficiently.

I have always been eclectic about my work approaches and using various modalities (e.g. NLP, Journaling, Affirmations, Mind Mapping , Bach Flower Remedies, and whatever I find effective in meeting the needs of my client) of healing and motivation. It has always been a search for me, of some better techniques and approaches to accelarate and accentuate the healing process in my clients.

In this ever-growing field of Psychology and Wellness, I always aim to keep abreast of all the developments and modalities of the art of healing.
